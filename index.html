What does following your curiosity
in the age of AI actually look like?

Curiosity in Action

She spent three weeks using AI to understand her grandmother's dementia medication, then wrote a 4-page letter to the consultant with questions he said no family member had ever asked.

Girl, 16 · Healthcare · Inquiry

Curiosity in Action

He got curious why 808 bass hits feel different in your chest, followed the thread from hip-hop to sound physics to Fourier transforms, and now produces his own tracks.

Boy, 15 · Music + Physics · Creative

Curiosity in Action · Builder

He built a dashboard that pulls weather and soil data to alert his da when conditions put cattle at risk. Never took a coding class.

Boy, 16 · Roscommon · Agriculture · Rural · Build

Curiosity in Action · Learning

Told she "wasn't a maths person," she got curious about why crypto crashes. Within six weeks she was teaching game theory to her own class.

Girl, 16 · Mathematics · Accelerated Learning

Curiosity in Action

He kept losing at FIFA, asked AI to explain the game's hidden mechanics, discovered he loves systems thinking, and now pulls apart bus timetables and league tables for fun.

Boy, 14 · Gaming + Analytics · Systems Thinking

Curiosity in Action · Builder

She built an app that translates HSE medical letters and tracks appointments for her Polish mam and three other families on her road.

Girl, 15 · Polish-Irish · Health Access · Build

Curiosity in Action

Furious about plastic waste, she used AI to map her neighbourhood's recycling gaps, designed a targeted poster campaign, and got two proposals adopted by the council.

Girl, 15 · Environment · Civic Advocacy

Curiosity in Action · Builder

His autistic brother melts down around homework. He built a visual timer app with calming colours and familiar characters. Three families in his area now use it.

Boy, 15 · Caregiving · Accessibility · Build

Section 4

ECHO5

A practical method for the age of AI

The five phases

E1

Encourage

"Make uncertainty safe."
Develops Concern
E2

Enlighten

"Show them the landscape honestly."
Develops Curiosity
E3

Empower

"Given what you've told me, what feels true?"
Develops Control
E4

Equip

"Show me what you made. Tell me what you learned."
Develops Confidence
E5

Envision

"What kind of person do you want to become?"
Integrates all four into Adaptability
